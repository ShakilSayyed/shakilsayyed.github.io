Common Plumbing and processing Basic Knowledge & Quiz§

Q-1 What plastic pipes are used in for sanitation, drainage and rain water ?§

Mainly PVC **Polyvinyl chloride**and ABS **Acrylonitrile butadiene styrene** are used for the above purposes. PVC comes in different types.
  1. CPVC: Chlorinated Polyvinyl chloride: They are produced by chlorination of PVC resin to make it more flexible than mere PVC, they can be used for both cold and hot water due to tolerating heat up to 180 degree Fahrenheit.
  2. PVCu: unplasticised Polyvinyl chloride:used for many purposes.
  3. PVCmu or MuPVC BS 5255:Modified unplasticised Polyvinyl chloride: used for solvent weld waste and overflow pipes.
  4. CuPVCChlorinated unplasticised Polyvinyl chloride: used for solvent weld cold water service pipe in the late 1970s. They have been replaced by new MDPE.
  • ABS: :can easily be recognized by its black color and rigidity and used for drainage lines, waste pipes.
  • Polypropylene: Widely used in plumbing industry used for waste pipes, cistern, WC siphons and overflow pipe work. It can not be solvent welded.

Q- What plastic pipes are used for cold and hot water supply and for central heating services ?§

1.PEX or PE:Cross linked polyethylene is a good for both hot and cold temperatures.

MDPE and HDPE are two kinds of PE that are used in water pipes. MDPE has superseded HDPE ( used in 1980s). They are hard wearing plastics for water and gas pipe fittings.
  1. PB or PB-1: Polybutylene: It has high temperature and pressure resistance, low noise transmission, low thermal expansion and low thermal transmission.
It does not suffer from corrosion or scaling and conforms to BS EN 1057. It is used in wet central heating system and underfloor heating.

Q- Metal pipes used in cold and hot water supply and central heating§

  1. Copper tube BS En 1057: They can last for fifty years as it ages it thins out leading to pinhole leaks. R220 and R250 are two commonly used copper pipes in cold and hot water pipes.
Copper tubes can be used for cold and hot water, central heating services, gas installation ( oxygen, methane and other gases ), oil system and sanitation systems.

  1. Chromium coated copper tube: they are used where aesthetics consideration to be made. And plastic coated copper known as kutelex is available where protection from the elements is essential.

  2. Galvanized Steel or Iron: Galvanized pipe is steel pipe that has been treated with zinc coating. This keeps the water away from corroding the pipe.

They were used in pre 60's building or houses, they have shortest span of life up to 40 years and eventually do get corroded.

Q- Identify the given pipes§

Leads pipes are no longer permitted to be used.

What are different methods of jointing the metal and plastic pressure pipes ?§

There are four main methods of jointing the pipes
  1. Compression: Used in copper and plastics, compression fittings which are made from plastic are known as Philmac fittings.
  2. Soldered : Only used in copper.
  3. Push fits : used in plastics.
  4. Press fits : Used in copper.

Fusion Welded: Large underground water mains use fusion welded fittings. Fittings and the pipe are welded together by heat created by electricity.

What is flaring or belling ?§

It is a process of shaping the pipes end by means of using tools.

How many types of compression joints are used ?§

There are two types:
  • Type A or Non- manipulative: do not require flaring and commonly used in water fittings.
  • Type B or Manipulative: mostly used for gas lines can also be found in water fittings.

Type A consist of compression nut, copper or brass O ring and a fitting body.

Where is soldered Capillary Joints used ?§

Commonly domestic plumbing used soft soldered joints. They are made up of two type of fittings:
  1. Integral solder ring: It has a bend of lead-free solder housed inside a raised ring on the fitting socket so extra solder is not needed and it gets melted when heated.
  2. End feed: It does not have solder in the fittings and does not contain a bend.

Solders that contain lead are not allowed on pipework used for either hot or cold water supplies

What does push fit consist of ?§

It consist of a main body, screw cap, spacer washer, an EPDM O ring and a metal grab with a metal end ring.
